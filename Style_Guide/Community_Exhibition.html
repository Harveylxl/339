Community Exhibition

CLIENT PROJECT · UI/UX DESIGN · UX RESEARCH

Introduction

Community Exhibition is a local art space located in the lobby of the new Vancouver Art Gallery. The space displays outstanding works by BC artists on different themes each month. Local artists can submit their artworks online that fit the monthly theme. The top 10 works voted for will be displayed in the Gallery's lobby for one month. ​

Ethnography

Data collection

Interview

We interviewed visitors about their visit to the gallery, how often they visited, and different ways they accessed information about the current exhibitions.

Online Survey

An online survey was created to gather additional quantitative information about people’s experiences when they previously visited.

Zoom Meeting

We did a group interview with Vancouver Art Gallery’s Director of Strategic Communications & Branding and Director of Publishing & Content Strategy.

Design Focus

How might we create a sense of community and identity within the Vancouver Art Gallery that can be felt by regular and first-time visitors?

Persona

Because our research insight is centred on promoting exhibition marketing, our main targeted audience is frequent visitors with experience in artistic creation and first-time visitors.

User Journey Map

Muhan Huang assumed the responsibility of designing the journey map, while other team members contributed to the content creation. Based on our previous research and our personas, I responsible for mapping out the journey they may go through before, during and after their visit at Vancouver Art Gallery.

Storyboard

Terrence Xu took the role of developing the storyboard. He presented a visually captivating narrative that revolves around the journey of an artist and the exploration of local artwork by a visitor.

Participatory Workshop

The workshop was led by Andy, with collaborative content creation from all team members. The objective of this workshop was to bring together individuals from diverse roles and engage in intensive exploration and discussion to generate potential solutions.

Reflection

After completing the initial mockup, I used Figma to create a prototype with the most important process for user testing. This process allows us to step back and understand the user experience as a beginner. I updated the submission page of Community Exhibition based on feedback from potential users to provide a more user-friendly interface for artists to submit their work.
